## Community Guidelines

To maintain a positive and helpful community, please follow our guidelines:

### ✅ Do's
- Be respectful and kind to all members
- Search before posting to avoid duplicates
- Provide context when asking questions
- Mark helpful answers in Q&A discussions
- Share knowledge generously
- Respect exam NDAs
- Use appropriate categories
- Celebrate others' successes

### ❌ Don'ts
- Share actual exam questions or content
- Post spam or promotional content
- Harass or insult other members
- Share exam dumps or unauthorized materials
- Post off-topic content
- Plagiarize without attribution

**Full Guidelines:** [Community Moderation Guidelines](https://github.com/pmady/golden-kubestronaut-learning/blob/main/.github/MODERATION_GUIDELINES.md)

## Exam NDA Policy

All CNCF and Linux Foundation certifications require signing a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA).

### ✅ You CAN Share:
- General exam format and structure
- Study resources and strategies
- Topics covered (from official curriculum)
- Overall difficulty and experience
- Time management tips
- General advice

### ❌ You CANNOT Share:
- Specific exam questions or scenarios
- Screenshots or recordings
- Detailed task descriptions
- Exact question wording
- Any proprietary exam materials

**Violations may result in:**
- Content removal and discussion ban
- Report to certification authorities
- Potential certification revocation
