### Prerequisites Checklist

#### Technical Requirements
- [ ] **Operating System**: Windows 10+, macOS 10.13+, or Linux
- [ ] **Browser**: Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari (latest version)
- [ ] **Internet**: Stable connection (5 Mbps+ recommended)
- [ ] **Webcam**: Required for online proctoring
- [ ] **Microphone**: Required for online proctoring
- [ ] **Screen Resolution**: 1024x768 minimum

#### Environment Setup
- [ ] **Quiet Room**: Private space without interruptions
- [ ] **Clean Desk**: Only allowed items visible
- [ ] **No Dual Monitors**: Single monitor only
- [ ] **No Virtual Machines**: Physical machine required
- [ ] **Admin Rights**: For exam software installation

#### Account Preparation
- [ ] **CNCF Account**: Created at [cncf.io](https://www.cncf.io/)
- [ ] **PSI Account**: Created at [psionline.com](https://www.psionline.com/)
- [ ] **Email Access**: Verified email address
- [ ] **Payment Method**: Credit card or PayPal ready

## üìÖ Step-by-Step Booking Process

### Step 1: Choose Your Exam
1. Visit [CNCF Certification Page](https://www.cncf.io/certification/)
2. Select your desired certification
3. Review exam objectives and requirements
4. Check for any prerequisites

### Step 2: Create PSI Account
1. Go to [PSI Exams](https://www.psionline.com/)
2. Click "Create Account"
3. Fill in personal information
4. Use the same email as your CNCF account
5. Verify your email address

### Step 3: Schedule Your Exam
1. Log into your PSI account
2. Click "Schedule Exam"
3. Search for your CNCF certification
4. Select "Online Proctored" option
5. Choose your preferred date and time
6. Confirm time zone settings
7. Complete payment process

### Step 4: Confirmation and Preparation
1. Save confirmation email
2. Add exam to calendar
3. Download exam software (if required)
4. Complete system check
5. Review exam rules and policies

## üïê Best Scheduling Times

### Optimal Time Slots
- **Morning Slots (9 AM - 12 PM)**: Fresh mind, less fatigue
- **Mid-Week (Tuesday-Thursday)**: Better support availability
- **Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, holidays

### Time Zone Considerations
- **Check Your Local Time**: Confirm in booking confirmation
- **International Students**: Consider time zone differences
- **Daylight Saving**: Account for time changes

### Preparation Timeline
- **6-8 Weeks Out**: Book exam date
- **4-6 Weeks Out**: Intensive study phase
- **2-4 Weeks Out**: Practice exams and weak areas
- **1-2 Weeks Out**: Final review and system check
- **1 Week Out**: Light review, rest, and preparation

## üîÑ Rescheduling and Cancellation

### Rescheduling Process
1. Log into PSI account
2. Go to "My Exams"
3. Click "Reschedule"
4. Select new date/time
5. Pay rescheduling fee (if applicable)

### Rescheduling Fees
- **48+ hours notice**: No fee
- **24-48 hours notice**: $50 fee
- **Less than 24 hours**: Full exam price

### Emergency Cancellations
- **Medical Emergency**: With documentation, may qualify for refund
- **Technical Issues**: Contact support immediately
- **Personal Emergency**: Case-by-case basis
